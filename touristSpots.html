Top Destinations in the Philippines

Discover the beauty and adventure that awaits you in the Philippines. Here are some of the must-visit destinations across the archipelago.

Baguio

Known as the Summer Capital of the Philippines, Baguio offers cool weather, scenic parks, and cultural heritage sites.

Learn More
Boracay

Famous for its powdery white-sand beaches and vibrant nightlife, Boracay is a top destination for relaxation and fun.

Learn More
La Union

A surfer's paradise, La Union is known for its stunning beaches, laid-back vibe, and vibrant food scene.

Learn More
Banaue Rice Terraces

Often referred to as the "Eighth Wonder of the World," these terraces are a testament to ancient Filipino ingenuity and agricultural mastery.

Learn More
Tagaytay

With its breathtaking view of Taal Volcano and cool climate, Tagaytay is a favorite weekend getaway for city dwellers.

Learn More
Cebu

The Queen City of the South offers rich history, amazing beaches, and vibrant festivals like Sinulog.

Learn More
Palawan

Known for its limestone cliffs and crystal-clear waters, Palawan is home to El Nido and the Puerto Princesa Underground River.

Learn More
Siargao

Siargao is the surfing capital of the Philippines, offering stunning islands, lagoons, and a relaxed vibe.

Learn More
Vigan

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, Vigan is known for its preserved Spanish colonial and Asian architecture.

Learn More
Batanes

The northernmost province of the Philippines, Batanes is famous for its rolling hills, stone houses, and scenic views.

Learn More
