Baguio - The Summer Capital of the Philippines

Known for its cool climate, rich culture, and stunning natural landscapes, Baguio is a must-visit destination.

Highlights of Baguio

  • Visit Burnham Park and enjoy a serene boat ride.
  • Explore the historic Camp John Hay and its picturesque surroundings.
  • Discover local arts and culture at the BenCab Museum.
  • Stroll through the vibrant and beautiful Botanical Garden.

Baguio is not just about its parks; it offers a rich tapestry of culture and tradition. Itâ€™s an ideal escape from the tropical heat, especially during the summer months.

A Brief History

Baguio was established as a hill station during the American colonial period. The city was designed to provide a cool respite from the heat of the lowlands. Today, it boasts a unique blend of historical significance and modernity, making it a cultural hub in the Philippines.

Tips for Travelers

  • Pack Layers: Baguio's temperature can vary throughout the day, so wear layers to stay comfortable.
  • Try Local Cuisine: Don't miss out on the famous Baguio strawberries and other local delicacies.
  • Respect the Culture: Baguio is home to various indigenous communities. Be respectful and mindful of their traditions.
